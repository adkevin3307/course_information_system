教材內容
中文 English
「船舶管理」旨在以船舶為一商業系統, 介紹船舶如何從出生到茁壯、營運，重點包括船舶設計、製造、組合、安裝、測試、交船、品保、營運、及海洋環保等一系列基本觀念，及船東、船廠、與船級協會在船舶商業系統中所扮演之角色。特別著重經濟觀點解析船舶之供需關係，船東需求船價及船舶生產成本如何達成市場價格及預測，並對未來世界航運及造船市場發展作一分析，使學生充份掌握世界海運之動脈。 The main emphasis of the course is to provide a more systematic introduction for the theory of shipboard management in view of a business system. The contents include ship design, production management, quality management, shipping management. Econometric techniques are used to analyze how to obtain the ship market price among the contradiction of owner''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''s requirements, shipyard production cost, and classification society requirements. Finally, the analysis of competitive advantage for shipbuilding industry will be made so that let student sufficiently possess the dynamics for shipbuilding industry.
